The costs of ALSA are high if you plan to coaling only few samples per year. We are introducing "Coalignment as a Service", an innovative solution aimed at democratizing access to precise and efficient sample preparation. Through this service, research institutions and laboratories can ask us to coalign crystals for a specified fee, enabling them to utilize cutting-edge automation without the upfront investment in equipment. It is a cost-effective and flexible alternative for laboratories that require high-quality sample preparation for their experiments, ensuring access to advanced technology tailored to their specific needs.

Easy scaling of the number of crystals

Laboratories often coalign hundreds of samples by hand. For a successful experiment thousands of samples will be much better. If you can grow the crystals, we can coalign them.

How long will it take

Usually coaligned samples (500-2000 crystals) can be achieved within 14 days (including machine tuning and optimization).

Service will include:

  • quality check of all crystals
  • glueing
  • mosaicity analysis of the glued crystals
  • detailed logbook of the whole process
